Critter Pit: Humble Beginnings




How to Play



  • Select a tank (Turtle, Lion), a support (Dove, Donkey), an attacker (Fox, Wolf) and a fourth critter to come off the bench.

  • Each critter has 4 unique and powerful abilities (Click on critter or critter's portrait to select a critter)
    Abilities fall under 3 types: (Select ability then select target(s))
    • Block: Self-cast, generally increases the critter's defence
    • Attack: If the target is behind another critter it hits the front critter instead of its initial target
    • Support: Unrestricted - read descriptions

  • Aside from abilities, critters may move and may also perform bench actions (Select critter and select desired position to move to)

  • TIP: Move your tank to be in front of the critters you want to protect, then use a block ability to incoming attacks

  • Critters cannot attack or be attacked on the bench, but have a passive ability related to the bench and gain 30 energy at the end of each turn compared to the normal 10

  • One critter can be on the bench at a time, at least one critter must be on the field, and maximum 3 can be on the field at any given time

  • Items are instant 1 time use actions(use items by dragging their picture onto the action bar)

  • All actions show up on the action bar, rearrange your turn by dragging the blocks around and delete an action by dragging it off the action bar

  • NOTE: Items take effect before abilities when they are triggered at the same time

  • Morale: Successful attacks increase your morale while reducing your enemy's. When actions from both players happen at the same second, a coinflip based on morale ratio decides which action gets performed first.
