About the Computer Science Enrichment Club

The Computer Science Enrichment Club is a student-run group that focuses on helping students with a passion for Computer Science take the next steps in their career. We focus on teaching the basics of competitive programming, introducing coding competitions and hackathons to students.

This 2016 Fall Term, CSEC will be held in IC402 every Friday from 4PM - 6PM. Be sure to join us!

LEARN

Learn C++ Programming, Competitive Programming techniques and algorithms, web design, source control, and more.

CREATE

Create amazing applications, games, and programs and explore other unique creations at multiple Hackathons all over North America.

DISCOVER

Network with other developers and programmers, meet new friends, and collaborate on personal projects together
